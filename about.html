About Our School

Samaritans School (Samaritans English Medium Higher Secondary School) in Hoshangabad (Narmadapuram) is a well-established CBSE co-educational day school, founded in 1996, known for its focus on holistic growth with strong academics, diverse extracurriculars (Sports, Arts, NCC, Scouts), and good facilities like libraries, labs, and transport, aiming to provide a comprehensive education through English medium from Nursery to Class 12 under the Ratlam Maruti Educational Society.

Samaritans School has grown significantly since its start with 36 students in 1996 to over 3200 students, becoming a prominent educational institution in the region. The school is also active on social media, celebrating events like NCC Day and connecting with alumni.
